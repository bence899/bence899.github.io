Code Culture (Society)

Code Culture was created to provide students with a community where the fundamental skills, practices are shared by experienced alumni students and senior students. The society provides 2 types of programs:

Lectures:

Mainly discussions held by alumni students giving advice and sharing their experiences of the real world to future generations

Tutorials:

Helping students more specifically with the theory side of the course itself. Anyone can help, the aim of society to form a friendly community where people can learn and improve.

I started Volunteering to hold tutorials for student and help them understand more about the uses of the computer science field.

  • Developing a skill to share resources with others and collaborate as a team.
  • Had to deal with unmotivated students and think of a way to explain to them the theory in a more creative and interesting way.
